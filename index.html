Paddles Up

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Paddles Up is a free mobile app that helps pickleball players find courts and check live availability near them. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use Paddles Up:

  • Location data — We use your device's GPS to show courts near you and to verify you are physically at a court before allowing you to submit an availability report. We do not store your location history.
  • Anonymous user ID — When you use the app, we assign you an anonymous ID so you can save favorite courts and submit availability reports. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.
  • Availability reports — When you tap Open, Busy, or Full on a court, we save that report along with your anonymous ID, the time of the report, and your approximate GPS coordinates at the time of submission. This is used to verify you were at the court.
  • Favorite courts — We save which courts you have marked as favorites, linked to your anonymous ID.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not require you to create an account
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

How we use your information

  • To show you pickleball courts near your current location
  • To display live court availability to all users
  • To verify you are physically at a court before accepting an availability report
  • To save your favorite courts so you can access them quickly

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Availability reports automatically expire after 30 minutes. We retain anonymous usage data to improve the app.

Third party services

Paddles Up uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps — Map display. Privacy Policy
  • Expo — App framework. Privacy Policy

Children's privacy

Paddles Up is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will update the date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your rights

Since we collect only anonymous data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you would like your anonymous data removed, contact us and we will delete it.
